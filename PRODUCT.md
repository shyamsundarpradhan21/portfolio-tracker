# Product

## Register

product

## Users

A single user: the portfolio owner, a salaried professional (coal-sector PSU) and active investor managing six asset sleeves — Indian equity, US equity, mutual funds, fixed deposits, CMPF retirement corpus, and an algo-trading book. Checks the dashboard daily (often on mobile during market hours, on desktop in the evening) to see live net worth, sleeve performance, and deployment discipline. Deeply numerate; wants dense, truthful numbers, not simplified summaries.

## Product Purpose

A private, live net-worth dashboard ("wealth-os"). It marks every sleeve to market (NSE/NYSE quotes, MF NAVs, FD accrual, CMPF simulation), reconstructs ledger-true history, projects wealth to retirement (March 2055), and tracks savings rate from payslips. Success = one glance answers "where do I stand, what moved, am I on track."

## Brand Personality

Calm, precise, cinematic. The interface should feel like a professional trading terminal designed by a craftsman — quiet glass surfaces, hairline borders, restrained motion with spring easing (Emil Kowalski school). Confidence through accuracy, not decoration.

## Anti-references

- Generic SaaS fintech dashboards (gradient hero metrics, identical card grids, navy-and-gold).
- The previous version of this app: emoji icon buttons (✨🌗↻), cramped sticky header stuffed with cards.
- Retail broker apps that gamify (confetti, red/green overload, urgency cues).

## Design Principles

1. **Numbers are the interface** — typography and tabular alignment carry the hierarchy; chrome stays out of the way.
2. **Truth over comfort** — losses, approximations, and caveats are shown plainly (e.g. "off-NW", "indicative").
3. **Live, but calm** — price flashes and refreshes are subtle pulses, never alarms.
4. **Density with breathing room** — expert-level data density, but rhythm and whitespace keep it scannable.
5. **One user, zero onboarding** — no marketing surface, no empty-state selling; every screen assumes the owner.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

WCAG AA contrast on all text (verify especially muted grays on dark glass). Full keyboard navigation for tabs, sidebar, and drawer. `prefers-reduced-motion` honored on all animations (NW shimmer, drawer, toasts). Color never the sole carrier of gain/loss — pair with sign (+/−) and icons.
